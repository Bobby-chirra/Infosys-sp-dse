Key Preparation Points

Sleep Schedule

Plan assumes ~16 waking hours/day. Adjust timings based on your natural sleep pattern. Consistency is key for optimal performance.

Problem Selection

Focus on medium-difficulty problems that cover patterns rather than hard problems. Quality over quantity.

Language Adaptation

Practice with your interview language. The plan adapts problem counts and concepts for Java, Python, or JavaScript.

Mock Environment

Simulate real interview conditions: timed, no unnecessary help, talking through your thought process.

Active Recall

Review mistakes immediately. Create flashcards for patterns you struggle with. Teach concepts to reinforce learning.

STAR Method

For behavioral questions: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Prepare concise stories that highlight soft skills.
